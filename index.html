Background (1 of 2)

Personality Assessment

This 75 question personality assessment aims to measure 15 underlying personality traits using similar measurements to the Jackson Personality Inventory - Revised (JPI-R).

The JPI-R measures were selected due to the range of personality traits measured and inclusion of 7 measurements for “analytical” and “dependable,” traits previously reported in those performing highly technical work roles. This set of 15 measures has been useful in vocational counseling.

This JPI-R proxy consists of questions drawn from the public domain repository of personality questions (https://ipip.ori.org/).
